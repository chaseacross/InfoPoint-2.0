If the member is on both accounts, it is not necessary for the member to verify both account numbers prior to transferring funds.

  1. Verify the member (Contact Center / NFC/Lobby/Teller).
  2. Copy the member's Social Security number (SSN) from Member View.
  3. In DNA, click Services > Relationships.
  4. On the Main Menu bar, click Quick Inquiries > Relationship Profile.
  5. In Search Criteria, paste the member's Social Security number.
  6. Click Query.
  7. Click the withdrawal account number. Options will become available on the blue sub menu bar.
  8. Click Transfer.
  9. From the To Account drop-down, select the receiving account.
  10. Enter the amount to be transferred.
  11. From the To Account Roles drop-down, select the member requesting the transfer.
  12. Click Update.
  13. Verify the transaction with the member, including the following:
    • Partial account numbers
    • Transaction type
    • Amount
  14. Click Process.
  15. Contact Center: Provide a reference number to the member consisting of the first set of digits followed by your cashbox number.
  16. Lobby/NFC/Teller: Provide a receipt to member and complete a Withdrawal/Transfer form.
